Fried Rice Recipe

This is a quick and easy way to prepare a delicious home-cooked meal without the hustle.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Green Peas
  • Meat

Instructions

  1. Boil the rice for 15 minutes.
  2. Fry your onions till Golden Brown.
  3. Add the tomatoes, carrots and green peas.
  4. Add the rice, stir and let cook under low heat.
